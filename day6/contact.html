Frequently Asked Questions

How does TripCraft AI work?

Our AI analyzes your travel preferences, budget, and destination data to create personalized itineraries. It considers factors like weather, local events, transportation options, and your interests to optimize your trip.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption and never share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent. Your travel plans are private and secure.

Can I modify AI-generated plans?

Yes! All AI-generated itineraries are fully customizable. You can add, remove, or modify any activity, and our AI will automatically adjust the rest of your plan accordingly.

What destinations do you support?

TripCraft supports destinations worldwide. Our database includes over 10,000 cities and tourist destinations with detailed information about attractions, restaurants, and activities.

How accurate are budget estimates?

Our budget estimates are based on real-time data from thousands of travelers and local sources. We provide ranges and update prices regularly, but actual costs may vary based on your choices.

Do you offer customer support?

Yes! We offer 24/7 email support, live chat during business hours, and phone support for premium members. Our AI assistant is also available anytime for instant help.
